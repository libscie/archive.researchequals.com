Summary

This is a collection of supplementary material describing the project ALL-IN-META-BCG-CORONA, designed as a replication package for anyone that wants to reproduce our work or start a similar project. It accompanies the following paper:


Bacillus Calmette-Guérin vaccine to reduce COVID-19 infections and hospitalisations in healthcare workers – a living systematic review and prospective ALL-IN meta-analysis of individual participant data from randomised controlled trials

Summary

I collect works here that pertain to my work on Liberate Science GmbH, ResearchEquals and more. Broadly speaking, I curate pieces around publishing, meta-research, and how to reduce power imbalances in research.


This is a very broad topic, so it may prove to be a ride. Follow along if you'd like!


I may extend this description over time, as I start finding a more refined definition of what I'm curating.

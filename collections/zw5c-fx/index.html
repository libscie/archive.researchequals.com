PubCasts

PubCasts are annotated and abridged audiobook-style recordings of peer-reviewed literature, read to you by the author(s). This collection accepts PubCasts made by anyone following the PubCasts Protocol (Harrison and Loring 2021).

Summary

PubCasts are the creation of Dr. Hannah L. Harrison (Dalhousie University, Canada) and Dr. Philip A. Loring (University of Guelph, Canada) and offer researchers a way to share their work with broader audiences through a more accessible and engaging format.


Follow Harrison and Loring's PubCast Protocol (available on ResearchEquals) to learn how to make your own PubCast and add it to this collection.

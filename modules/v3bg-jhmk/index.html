Summary

This R code reproduces Figure 2 of the publication Klappe, Heijmans, Groen, Ter Schure, Cornet & De Keizer (2023) 'Correctly structured problem lists lead to better and faster clinical decision-making in electronic health records compared to non-curated problem lists: A single-blinded crossover randomized controlled trial' based on data available elsewhere (and linked here).

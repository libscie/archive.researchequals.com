Summary

At the Open Science Retreat (https://open-science-retreat.gitlab.io/) from April 3nd to April 7th 2023 in Kochel am See, Germany, we set out to explore management and leadership strategies for teams and collaborations in science. This effort was conducted as a series of semi-structured short interviews with participants. This module contains the questions that guided the interview

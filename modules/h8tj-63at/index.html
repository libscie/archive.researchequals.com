Summary

Twitter profiles of scientific journals in the field of Chemistry, as of March 2022.

The sample is based on the group 'Chemistry' in Web of Science's SCIE, which comprises 21 distinct categories (from Biochemical Research Methods to Spectroscopy). The data were collected manually in February/March 2022 (based on a Twitter profile search) and pertain to 1790 journals.

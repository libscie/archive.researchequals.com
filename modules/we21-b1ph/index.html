Art of Principled Negotiation (for junior researchers)

doi: 10.53962/we21-b1ph

Originally published on 2022-06-16 under a CC BY 4.0

Authors

  • Gerben ter Riet

Summary

A powerpoint presentation that may be used to organize workshops in which participants may practice with Principled Negotiation in their academic environment. One role play is elaborated and deals with honorary authorship, a questionable research practice that is very prevalent.

Summary

These are our design workshop slides for the Recognition & Rewards festival, to be held in Utrecht on 2023.04.13. This in-person design workshop aims to brainstorm elements for a research CV (existing or future) and then create rough sketches for each element in groups. Finally, participants bring together these different elements into different alternative CVs, which can serve as the basis for mock-ups to be utilized in the future.
